 Our vision:
 Utilizing artificial intelligence to serve Saudi Arabia’s 2030 economic vision. We believe in the ability of  artificial intelligence to solve youth 
 problems and make their lives easier. We also believe in its ability in improving and prolonging products that we use in different areas in our
 daily lives.





 Our message:
 Help employ international indicators for artificial intelligence along with Saudi’s 2030 vision. We also aim to increase the efficiency of the society
 through artificial intelligence and placing it as a national priority in development, innovation, education, and leadership in the field of artificial
 intelligence.
